How it works

Each world is procedurally generated from a fixed seed — the same mountains, roads and landmarks for every student, every visit. Walk (or run) between learning stations: study a unit's key ideas, then clear the station by scoring 70%+ on real exam-style questions drawn from that unit. A light beacon always marks a suggested next stop, the quest log tracks your mastery, and progress saves on your device.

Desktop: WASD + mouse · Shift to run · E to interact · Touch: joystick + drag to look.

Built for Mr. Maccarello's classes · runs entirely in your browser · nothing you type is ever sent anywhere.
